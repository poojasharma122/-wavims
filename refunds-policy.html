Refunds Policy

Wavims (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a hotel discovery and listings platform. We do not process hotel bookings or handle any payments for accommodations. As such, we do not offer refunds of any kind through this website.

Please read the following information carefully to understand how refunds are handled by third-party services.

1. No Direct Bookings

Wavims does not operate as a booking engine or travel agency. Our role is to:

  • List curated Las Vegas hotels

  • Direct users to official hotel websites or trusted booking platforms

All reservations, payments, and booking confirmations are completed externally. We do not collect any payment information or issue receipts for bookings.

2. Refunds Through Booking Platforms

If you booked a hotel after clicking a link on Wavims, your payment and reservation were processed through a third-party website or the hotel itself. For any refund requests, cancellation issues, or booking changes, you must contact the hotel or booking provider directly.

Each hotel or booking platform has its own refund and cancellation policy, which may vary depending on:

  • Room type or rate selected

  • Cancellation window

  • Booking terms and conditions

  • Promotional or non-refundable offers

We strongly recommend reviewing those policies before finalizing any reservation.

3. Affiliate Partnerships

Wavims may earn a commission if you book a hotel through one of our affiliate links. This does not affect the price you pay, nor does it involve us in your payment or booking process.

We are not responsible for refunds, disputes, or service issues related to third-party bookings.

4. Contacting Us

If you experience a problem with a hotel listed on our platform and believe it may relate to the way it was represented on Wavims, you may contact us for feedback or clarification.

However, we cannot issue refunds or intervene in payment disputes.
