About

Built for How People Actually Travel to Vegas

Wavims was created with one goal in mind — to make finding the right hotel in Las Vegas faster, easier, and more relevant. This city offers everything from high-stakes casino floors to spa retreats, pool parties, and quiet escapes. But most hotel sites sort by stars or price. We sort by experience.

Wavims helps travellers cut through the noise by grouping hotels into four simple, experience-based categories:

  • High-Stakes Hotels –For casino lovers and round-the-clock action

  • Golfing Getaways –For players who want green space and peace between rounds

  • Mind & Body Hotels –For those seeking rest, spa treatments, and wellness

  • Social Hotspots – For groups, nightlife seekers, and high-energy weekends

Each hotel on our site is handpicked based on location, quality, and how well it delivers on its experience. We don’t list every property in the city — just the ones that match what people are really looking for.

Why Wavims Works

Focused on Las Vegas Only

We don’t try to cover every destination — our platform is built entirely around Las Vegas. That means sharper insight, better hotel matches, and a smoother experience for travellers who already know where they’re headed.

No Filler, No Fluff

You won’t find 200 hotel listings here. We keep it tight, only including hotels that offer a strong fit for their category. Our content is clear, honest, and written for real people — not algorithms.

Built for Decision-Making

Whether you’re planning a solo golf weekend or a full-scale bachelor trip, Wavims gives you a head start. Every listing includes highlights, pricing, and a review you can actually use to compare options.

How We Make Money
