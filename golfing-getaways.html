Golfing Getaways

Golfing Getaways in Las Vegas

There’s more to Vegas than casinos. Just beyond the Strip are championship courses, desert fairways, and peaceful stays where the focus is green. These Golfing Getaways combine resort-style comfort with prime access to the best golf Las Vegas has to offer — from tee-off to wind-down.

Best Golf Hotels in Las Vegas

Wynn Las Vegas ★★★★★

Price per night: $329

  • Private 18-hole championship course
  • Five-star spa and fitness centre
  • On-site dining and luxury amenities

Wynn is the only resort on the Strip with a full 18-hole golf course on-site — and it’s as refined as the hotel itself. The lush, landscaped fairways are steps from your suite, with tee times reserved exclusively for guests. After a round, unwind at the spa or head straight to one of Wynn’s acclaimed restaurants.

The Westin Lake Las Vegas Resort & Spa ★★★★☆

Price per night: $219

  • Access to Reflection Bay Golf Club
  • Peaceful setting outside the Strip
  • Spa, pool, and lakeside views

Located about 25 minutes from the Strip, this resort is a hidden gem for golfers looking to escape the crowds. Guests enjoy priority access to Reflection Bay, a Jack Nicklaus Signature course. It’s quiet, comfortable, and made for low-key luxury.

JW Marriott Las Vegas Resort & Spa ★★★★☆

Price per night: $235

  • Steps from TPC Las Vegas
  • Sprawling resort with pool and gardens
  • Full-service spa and dining options

Set in the Summerlin area, this resort is ideal for golfers wanting easy access to TPC Las Vegas — a PGA Tour-level course. The JW Marriott offers a relaxed vibe, large rooms, and one of the most serene settings in the city. You’re away from the noise but still within reach of the action.

The Mirage ★★★★☆

Price per night: $199

  • Near Las Vegas National Golf Club
  • Tropical pool and wellness spa
  • Central Strip location

The Mirage offers solid comfort and convenience for golf travellers. It’s just a short drive from the historic Las Vegas National Golf Club, and the resort itself features a relaxed layout with plenty of space. The pool area, in particular, is a standout for post-golf downtime.

South Point Hotel Casino & Spa ★★★★☆

Price per night: $169

  • Close to Bali Hai Golf Club
  • Large rooms and free parking
  • Quiet location off the Strip

South Point sits at the southern end of Las Vegas Boulevard and offers easy access to Bali Hai — one of the Strip’s most popular courses. It’s quieter than central resorts but still packed with features: casino, spa, restaurants, and even a bowling centre.

The Signature at MGM Grand ★★★★☆

Price per night: $209

  • Minutes from Topgolf Las Vegas
  • Quiet, non-casino setting
  • All-suite accommodations

If you prefer calm between rounds, The Signature is a great choice. It’s set just behind MGM Grand and offers quiet, all-suite towers with full kitchens. Topgolf Las Vegas is within walking distance, and you're a short drive to several local courses.

Red Rock Casino Resort & Spa ★★★★☆

Price per night: $249

  • Near Arroyo and Bear’s Best golf courses
  • Scenic mountain views
  • Outdoor pool and high-end dining

Red Rock Resort is nestled near the Red Rock Canyon area, surrounded by several top-tier courses. It's a perfect base for golf and hiking, and the resort itself feels more like a private club — upscale but approachable.

Trump International Hotel Las Vegas ★★★★☆

Price per night: $229

  • Near Las Vegas Country Club and Wynn Golf Club
  • No casino — quiet atmosphere
  • Large suites with Strip views

This non-gaming hotel is tailored for guests who want space and quiet. You’re within 10 minutes of some of Vegas’s most popular courses and can enjoy a full suite with floor-to-ceiling windows. It’s a clean, efficient option for focused golf travel.
