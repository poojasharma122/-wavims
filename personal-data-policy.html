Personal Data Policy

Wavims (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Personal Data Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, how long we retain it, and your rights regarding your information.

This policy applies to all users who visit wavims.com (the “Site”) and interact with our services.

1. What Is Personal Data?

“Personal data” refers to any information that identifies you or can be used to identify you, either directly or indirectly. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name

  • Email address

  • IP address

  • Any personal details submitted via contact forms or newsletter signups

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We may collect personal data when you:

  • Fill out a contact form

  • Subscribe to our newsletter

  • Communicate with us via email

This may include:

  • Your name

  • Your email address

  • The content of your inquiry or message

  • Your communication preferences

We also collect certain non-personal data through cookies and tracking tools (see our Privacy Policy for full details).

3. Why We Collect Personal Data

We collect and process personal data to:

  • Respond to your messages and inquiries

  • Provide requested information or services

  • Send you newsletters and updates (if you opt in)

  • Improve our website and user experience

  • Maintain the functionality and security of the Site

We do not collect more data than necessary, and we do not use personal data for profiling or automated decision-making.

4. How We Store and Protect Personal Data

Your personal data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to keep your data safe.

5. How Long We Retain Personal Data

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. When we no longer need your information, we securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited situations:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating the Site (e.g., email platforms, analytics tools)

  • When required by law, regulation, or legal request

  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Wavims or our users

Any third parties with whom we share data are contractually obligated to handle it securely and in accordance with data protection standards.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you

  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data

  • Request deletion of your personal data (where applicable)

  • Withdraw consent for any marketing communications

  • Object to or restrict how your data is processed in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, email us at info@wavims.com.

8. Data Transfers

Wavims is operated from Canada. By using our Site, you acknowledge that your personal data may be stored or processed in Canada or other jurisdictions where our service providers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any such data transfers.
