Mind & Body Hotels

Las Vegas isn’t always about bright lights and busy streets. Some stays are built for stillness. These Mind & Body Hotels are perfect for travellers looking to recharge, recover, or simply unplug for a while. Think spa suites, thermal pools, wellness menus, and spaces designed for full-body calm.

Best Spa Hotels in Las Vegas

Waldorf Astoria Las Vegas ★★★★★

Price per night: $369

  • Tranquil spa with private suites and herbal steam
  • No casino for a quieter atmosphere
  • Skyline views and in-room wellness options

The Waldorf Astoria is one of the most peaceful luxury hotels in Vegas. The absence of a casino creates a serene atmosphere, and the spa is designed with relaxation in mind — from vitality pools to meditation spaces. It’s a top pick for those who want city views without the sensory overload.

Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas ★★★★★

Price per night: $349

  • Five-star spa with treatments for body and mind
  • Located on top floors of Mandalay Bay
  • Quiet, private, and tailored for relaxation

This property blends into the Strip without the buzz. Four Seasons sits atop Mandalay Bay but operates as its own peaceful escape. The spa offers deep massage, hot stone therapy, and facials — all backed by impeccable service and no gaming distractions.

Vdara Hotel & Spa ★★★★☆

Price per night: $259

  • Full-service spa and wellness centre
  • Non-gaming, non-smoking hotel
  • Steps from ARIA and Bellagio

Vdara is sleek, quiet, and spa-focused. From its eucalyptus steam room to customized treatments, this hotel is made for downtime. The lack of a casino makes it feel calmer than most Strip hotels, yet you're just a few steps from the city if you want it.

The Venetian Resort ★★★★☆

Price per night: $289

  • Canyon Ranch spa with 100+ services
  • Spacious suites for comfort
  • Full wellness menu including fitness and nutrition

The Venetian isn’t just about grandeur — its Canyon Ranch spa is one of the largest in the world. From hydrotherapy to acupuncture, this space covers the full wellness spectrum. It’s perfect for guests who want options — both on the treatment table and off.

Red Rock Casino Resort & Spa ★★★★☆

Price per night: $249

  • Spa treatments with mountain views
  • Outdoor lap pool and quiet atmosphere
  • Ideal for couples and solo retreats

Located near Red Rock Canyon, this resort has space, fresh air, and a slower pace. The spa includes a range of desert-inspired treatments, and the overall feel is more retreat than resort. Great for anyone looking to combine wellness with a taste of the outdoors.

  • Award-winning spa with steam, sauna, and salon
  • Outdoor relaxation areas

This off-Strip hotel delivers a more local experience with standout spa services. It’s perfect for low-key weekends and stress-free travel. The treatments are thorough, and the staff go out of their way to maintain a relaxing flow.

Delano Las Vegas ★★★★☆

Price per night: $239

  • BATHHOUSE Spa with modern design
  • All-suite hotel connected to Mandalay Bay
  • Stylish and quiet environment

Delano delivers a spa-focused stay without being far from the Strip. Its BATHHOUSE Spa is designed with minimalist interiors and top-tier treatments. The suites are large, and the vibe is low-key — ideal for a reset without leaving the action entirely behind.

Trump International Hotel Las Vegas ★★★★☆

Price per night: $229

  • Calm, non-gaming hotel with in-house spa
  • Spacious rooms with deep soaking tubs
